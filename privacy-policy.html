Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 19, 2026

Tyler Ontiveros ("we," "us," or "our") operates AI-powered communication services for small businesses. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with our services, including SMS/text messaging.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with our services via SMS or phone, we may collect:

  • Phone number — to send and receive messages
  • Message content — the texts you send and receive through our service
  • Appointment and booking details — dates, times, and service preferences you share
  • Name — if provided during conversation
  • Interaction timestamps — when messages are sent and received

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer service
  • Schedule and confirm appointments
  • Send appointment reminders and follow-ups
  • Improve our services and response quality

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

We may share information only:

  • With the business you are communicating with (our client) to fulfill your request
  • With service providers who help us operate (e.g., Twilio for messaging, cloud hosting) under strict confidentiality agreements
  • When required by law or to protect rights and safety

4. SMS/Text Messaging

  • Opt-in: By texting our service number, you consent to receive SMS messages related to your inquiry
  • Message frequency: Message frequency varies based on your interactions. Typically 1-10 messages per conversation
  • Opt-out: Reply STOP at any time to stop receiving messages
  • Help: Reply HELP for assistance
  • Message and data rates may apply depending on your carrier and plan
  • We do not share your phone number or SMS opt-in/consent data with third parties for marketing purposes

5. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services, typically no longer than 12 months after your last interaction. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit and at rest. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of communications at any time

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
