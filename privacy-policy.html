This Privacy Policy describes how Toro Intelligence ("we," "us," or "our"), operated by Tyler Ontiveros (Sole Proprietor), collects, uses, and protects information when you use our AI Receptionist SMS service.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with our SMS service, we may collect:

  • Phone number: Your mobile phone number when you text our service number.
  • Message content: The text messages you send to and receive from our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries about the business

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, share, rent, or trade your phone number, message content, or opt-in consent data to any third parties for marketing or promotional purposes.

We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With the business you contacted: Your messages and appointment details are shared with the specific business you are communicating with through our service.

  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or legal process.

4. Data Retention

We retain your message history and contact information for up to 12 months after your last interaction. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing toro956466@gmail.com.

5. Your Rights

  • Opt out: Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe immediately.
  • Get help: Reply HELP to receive assistance information.
  • Data deletion: Email us at toro956466@gmail.com to request deletion of your data.
  • Access: You may request a copy of the data we hold about you.

7. Children's Privacy
